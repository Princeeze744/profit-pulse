üìã How to Use Profit Pulse

  1. Prepare a CSV file with your trading data (see format below)
  2. Click "Choose File" and select your CSV file
  3. Click "Upload and Analyze" to process your trades
  4. View your performance metrics in the report section
  5. Use the "Analyze Existing Trades" button to analyze data already in Airtable

üìù CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file must have these columns (in any order):

  • Asset - The traded instrument (e.g., AAPL, EUR/USD, BTC)
  • Date - Trade date in YYYY-MM-DD format
  • Entry_Price - Price when you entered the trade
  • Exit_Price - Price when you exited the trade
  • Quantity - Trade size/volume
  • Type - Long or Short (optional, defaults to Long)

üí± Forex Trading Example

For currency pairs, use standard notation and appropriate decimal places:

GBP/USD,2023-10-02,1.3150,1.3100,15000,Short
